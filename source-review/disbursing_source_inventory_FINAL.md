## Cross-Source Conflict Register Summary

- **DA appointment authority remains unresolved.** The UPDATED conflicts register preserves three competing pieces of evidence: DDO Ops says Disbursing Agents must be appointed by the DO; Training 37.1 narrative says DA appointment by contingency commander while its slide says DO; and the Cashier Appointment scenario says the DA initiates cashier appointment while its excerpt says the DO appoints cashiers. Citations: [44_ 36. DDO Ops.pdf, PDF pp.20,25]; [41_ Appointment of Agents.pdf, PDF pp.3,9]; [205_ A1110_Cashier_Appt.pdf, PDF pp.1-2].
- **Check-cashing policy approval nuance remains unresolved.** The scenario action says check-cashing policies are set by the local commander, while the embedded excerpt requires written check-cashing policy approved by theater commander or designee. Citation: [406_ Check_Cashing.pdf, PDF pp.1-2].
- **Cash-count method remains context-specific.** The UPDATED register preserves a conflict/variation between three-count and four-count approaches, with DD Form 1081 count matching as a separate assessment standard. Citation: [206_ A1111_Cashier_Advance.pdf, PDF pp.1,3].
- **Cashier advance duplicate/variant remains unresolved.** `206_ A1111_Cashier_Advance.pdf` and `395_ A2300_Cashier_Advance.pdf` cover materially similar facts but cite different dates/framing. Citations: [206_ A1111_Cashier_Advance.pdf, PDF pp.1-2]; [395_ A2300_Cashier_Advance.pdf, PDF pp.1-3].
- **Counterfeit U.S. currency versus foreign-currency handling remains unresolved.** `216_ A1114_Counterfeit_Bill.pdf` supports U.S. counterfeit/altered currency handling, but it does not resolve the foreign-currency counterfeit scenario in `361_ A2311_Counterfeit_FC.pdf`. Citations: [216_ A1114_Counterfeit_Bill.pdf, PDF pp.1-3]; [361_ A2311_Counterfeit_FC.pdf, PDF pp.1-5].
- **Revaluation loss versus Average Purchase Rate must remain separate.** `276_ A2312a_Confused_Agent.pdf` supports revaluation-loss processing, while `277_ A2312b_Confused_Agent.pdf` supports Average Purchase Rate recomputation; these are not interchangeable. Citations: [276_ A2312a_Confused_Agent.pdf, PDF pp.1-3]; [277_ A2312b_Confused_Agent.pdf, PDF pp.1-4].
