# Curriculum Philosophy

## Primary Training Outcome

**Outcome:** The member can manually balance cash and explain the result.

**Authority Level:** Training practice supported by assessment exercises.

**Statement Type:** Analyst recommendation based on recurring accountability, cashier-advance, reconciliation, and accountability-validation requirements.

**Supporting Evidence**
- Cashiers count funds before accepting accountability. (`206_ A1111_Cashier_Advance.pdf`, pp.1–3)
- DD Form 1081 accountability must reconcile between issuer and recipient. (`206_ A1111_Cashier_Advance.pdf`, p.3)
- Manual accountability records appear throughout cashier, agent, loss, and foreign-currency scenarios. (`395_ A2300_Cashier_Advance.pdf`, pp.1–3; `276_ A2312a_Confused_Agent.pdf`, pp.1–3)

# Individual Learning Requirement Before Team Mission Access

## Requirement

**Statement Type:** Analyst recommendation.

Learners should complete individual instruction and demonstrate basic proficiency before participating in team accountability scenarios.

### Rationale

The reviewed evidence consistently evaluates individual accountability, appointments, advances, safeguarding responsibilities, cash counts, voucher review, and foreign-currency calculations before introducing team coordination.

Supporting Evidence:
- Cashier appointment and accountability (`205_ A1110_Cashier_Appt.pdf`, pp.1–3)
- Paying agent appointment (`210_ A1134_PA_Appt.pdf`, pp.1–4)
- Cashier advance (`206_ A1111_Cashier_Advance.pdf`, pp.1–3)
- Agent accountability and turn-ins (`46_ 37.5.1 & 37.16 Agent Accountability & Turn-ins.pdf`, pp.1–16)

# Remediation

## Remediation Trigger

Statement Type: Analyst recommendation.

Remediate when a learner:

- Cannot explain accountability.
- Cannot reconcile balances.
- Selects the wrong foreign-currency process.
- Fails to identify duplicate payments.
- Fails to identify counterfeit controls.

### Remediation Method

1. Repeat worked example.
2. Perform guided calculation.
3. Complete second constructed-response explanation.
4. Re-demonstrate accountability.

Evidence Basis:
- Validation standards in reviewed scenarios:
  - 206_ A1111_Cashier_Advance.pdf p.3
  - 216_ A1114_Counterfeit_Bill.pdf p.3
  - 276_ A2312a_Confused_Agent.pdf pp.2–3
  - 277_ A2312b_Confused_Agent.pdf p.2
