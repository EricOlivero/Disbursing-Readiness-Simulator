# Team Assignment Guide

## Purpose

The Facilitator view includes a Seven-route mission assignment board. It prevents a room full of teams from receiving the same problem and makes each team responsible for a different operational decision.

## Use

1. Enter each team name.
2. Select the team experience mix: developing, mixed, or experienced.
3. Select Auto-assign routes.
4. Review the mission focus beside each team.
5. Lock the board before announcing assignments.
6. Copy the launch list for the facilitator brief.

With seven or fewer named teams, the automatic assignment uses each mission at most once. With more than seven teams, the board warns that repeats are necessary.

## Seven Routes

1. M-01 Cashier Stand-Up: DD Form 1081 and opening accountability.
2. M-02 Paying Agent Market: payment execution and an overage.
3. M-03 Exchange Point: exchange controls and balanced closeout.
4. M-04 Revaluation Shift: revaluation-loss decision.
5. M-05 Average Purchase Rate: APR calculation and documentation.
6. M-06 Voucher Control Cell: duplicate-payment control and a shortage.
7. M-07 Accountability Interruption: continuity and witnessed transfer.

## Anti-Copy Controls

- Nearby teams should receive different missions.
- Team members must complete role-owned handoffs.
- The FRAGO is released only after the initial issue is accepted.
- Closeout outcomes differ across the mission set.
- Each role personally confirms evidence in the pass-device closeout.

Assignments are stored locally and do not transmit team names to a server.
