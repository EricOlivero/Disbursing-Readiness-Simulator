# Unit Pilot Feedback Guide

## Purpose

Use the built-in debrief after the individual and team journeys. It captures actionable training and interface feedback without collecting names, units, email addresses, or operational details.

## Ratings

Participants rate:

1. Instruction clarity.
2. Challenge level.
3. Mission realism.
4. Form-practice usefulness.
5. Confidence gained.

## Required Written Responses

- Exact confusion point.
- One change before the next training day.

Optional prompts capture the strongest learning moment and technical friction.

## Facilitator Use

1. Collect one response from each learner, team lead, facilitator, or observer.
2. Copy the facilitator summary after the event.
3. Review the average ratings and recurring friction themes.
4. Convert the top three themes into specific backlog items.
5. Clear local responses after preserving the sanitized summary.

Do not enter PII, unit identifiers, operational details, vendor data, accounting classifications, or other sensitive information.
