# Facilitator Run-of-Show

The Facilitator view includes a persistent timer for the full 180-minute event.

## Phase 1: Academy - 60 Minutes

- Learners work individually.
- Coach the mental model, not the answer.
- Confirm learners can explain what each record proves.
- Do not release qualification until the no-reference recall gate is complete.

## Phase 2: Qualification - 35 Minutes

- Protect individual work.
- Require the member to count, reconcile, and explain the result.
- Use remediation when the learner cannot explain the accountability effect.

## Phase 3: Team Ops - 70 Minutes

- Assign different missions to nearby teams.
- Enforce role ownership and pass-device handoffs.
- Release the FRAGO only after the initial issue is accepted.
- Observe communication, evidence use, and accountability continuity.

## Phase 4: AAR - 15 Minutes

- Ask what changed.
- Ask which evidence drove the decision.
- Ask how the team protected accountability.
- Record one retraining action.

The timer is a pacing aid only. It does not unlock content, change scores, or reveal answer keys. Its state is stored only on the local device.
